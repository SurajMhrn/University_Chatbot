GUIDELINES FOR RESIDENT STUDENTS
UG. PG & PhD PROGRAMMES
WELCOME TO STUDENTS’ RESIDENCE

XIM University Bhubaneswar new campus offers
programs for Undergraduate, Post-graduate and PhD
students. The residence plays a significant role in
developing the intellectual, social and cultural aspects
of life. It is in the residence that the students coming
from various parts of India and abroad find their home
away from home. Hence, the objective of the XIM
University residences is not only to provide adequate
facilities but also to create an atmosphere conducive to
forming successful, mature, socially responsible and
environment- friendly global leaders. The campus
discipline, guidelines and supervision are directed
towards this purpose. As resident scholars, it is up to
each one of you to make the best use of facilities and to
help one another in achieving the objectives of your stay
and to study well at XIM University.
The XIM University student residences, are primarily
a community. As a community, you are expected not
only to receive the benefits of the community but also
to contribute towards its welfare. Learning from and
contributing to enrich the culture and the spirit of XIM
University can be one of the most formative factors
during your stay here. It is a period of golden years in
your life. Relationships are formed that could last a
lifetime. Adaptations are made that widen your
flexibility. XIM University has a work culture that is
informal, formative, and conducive to growth as a
disciplined and a mature human being.

INTRODUCTION
On campus residence facilities are provided by the
university to enhance academic performance of
students. Attendance in academic sessions is
compulsory for all residents while they are within the
university premises.
Living in the student’s residence requires you to take
responsibility for your actions and their impact on the
experience of others with whom you live. This makes it
essential to observe certain rules and regulations and
to adhere to the code of student rights and
responsibilities.
Staying in the UG Residence is not mandatory for
Undergraduate students. The Residence facility is a
service given by the University for study and rest to
the deserving outstation students coming from far-off
places. Attendance in the academic sessions is
mandatory for residents when they are on campus. The
residence will remain closed from 9.30 AM to 4.30 PM
on working days. Only residents with permission from
their respective Deans and RMT are allowed to stay in
the residence during this time period of 9.30 AM to 4.30
PM. Residents missing classes without permission while
being in residence invite disciplinary action as per the
guidelines of the residence.
